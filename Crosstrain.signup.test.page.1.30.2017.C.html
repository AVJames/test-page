ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Want to get out of your building, meet more of the S.L.U. team, and increase you learning opportunities?

This program was developed to offer those opportunities. At Avisys, we know how important it is to both increase training opportunities and stay connected. The individuals that make up our team come from a myriad of professional backgrounds adn support customers with ranging needs and expectations. We don't believe that you should restrict your training and experiences to one location, team, or customer base.
